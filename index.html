Monkey

Monkeys use grooming to strengthen relationship, and the South American Titi monkey mate for life. Also spider monkeys are very sociable.

  • Scientific Name: Cercopithecidae
  • Average Length: 5.4 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 21 Years
  • Habitat: Tropical rainforests

Baby monkeys are adults from 3-4 years, also the largest monkey in the world is a Mandrill, it weighs 77 pounds and is 3 feet long. The smallest monkey in the world are Pygmy Marmosets.
